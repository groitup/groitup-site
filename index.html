Who We Are

A team that
believes in

Groitup is a dynamic media production and web development company committed to helping brands grow through creative, impactful digital solutions.

Founded by Abuzar Sharief, we specialise in video editing, event handling, and website development tailored to the unique needs of each client. With a passion for storytelling and design, we bring visions to life.

Our team is co-led by Syed Hannan, our Co-founder & Managing Director, who ensures every project runs smoothly and meets the highest standards. At Groitup, we build experiences that elevate your brand.
